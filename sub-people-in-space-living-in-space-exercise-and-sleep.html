What happens if you spend months being weightless? There would be no walking to the shops or riding a bike around the neighbourhood. You wouldn't even have to lift yourself up off a comfy sofa!

What would happen if you did no exercise in space? Your muscles would get weaker and weaker, so when you returned to Earth you wouldn't be able to stand up. To make sure they don't waste away, people in space have to exercise.

If you were in space, you would lift weights, run on treadmills and ride exercise bikes. Since you are weightless, you would have to tie yourself to your equipment with bungee cords and springs. You don't want to float off the treadmill during your morning jog!

Astronaut Sunita Williams has even run a marathon while strapped on to a treadmill! She ran 42 kilometres while circling the world almost four times!
