Weightlessness

Imagine weighing nothing! Weightlessness in space is a unique sensation. You can literally float around your spacecraft.

Why are you weightless? It isn't because of a lack of gravity. Instead, gravity is pulling your spacecraft and you in exactly the same direction. You would get the same sensation if you were in a rapidly falling elevator, which isn't recommended! You can also get the same sensation at the very top of a jump from a trampoline or diving board.

There are risks in weightlessness though, particularly on a long trip. Muscles and bones weaken without the regular exercise provided by moving around in Earth's gravity. So you may want to go for a jog or hop on to the space station exercise bike!
