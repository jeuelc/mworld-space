Where will people travel to next in space?

The most tantalising destination is Mars. It is the planet in our solar system that most resembles our home. Mars has valleys, mountains, and ice caps. Once upon a time Mars had lakes, rivers and seas too, and perhaps even living things.

However, the trip to Mars is expensive, long and dangerous. It would take years to travel to and from Mars, and as one travels away from the Earth, the radiation from the Sun could prove deadly. That's why we've sent probes and robots instead of astronauts.

Another possible destination is a nearby asteroid. This would be cheaper, shorter and safer than a trip to Mars. We may even be able to "bag" a small asteroid and bring it closer to Earth for closer examination by scientists!
