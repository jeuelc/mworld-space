The Soviet Union launched the first satellite and man into space and was very proud of these successes during the 'Cold War'. This was a time when things were pretty tense between the Soviet Union and America they didn't trust each other at all!

The United States felt it had to beat the Soviet Union in the Space Race. Apart from national prestige, the US was worried that the Soviet Union could use space for military or economic advantage.

In 1962, US President John F Kennedy laid down a challenge to land on the moon. It was now a race - a race to be finished in less than a decade.
