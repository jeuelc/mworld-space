There are two ways to get into space. Citizens of some nations can apply to be astronauts. Thousands of people apply to become astronauts, and only a handful are accepted. If you want to be an astronaut, don't forget to do your homework, as a university degree is essential! Many astronauts are test pilots, engineers or medical doctors. However, astronomers, geologists, meteorologists, oceanographers, politicians, and a teacher have also flown in space. You'll also need to train for years before your first flight.

However, there is now another way into space. Some people fly into space as tourists. To get into orbit isn't cheap, and may set you back tens of millions of dollars! But flights into space are getting cheaper and private company Virgin Galactic has already taken deposits from eager would-be space tourists queuing up for the chance to spend a few moments at the edge of space! If you had a few hundred thousand dollars, would you like to join them?
