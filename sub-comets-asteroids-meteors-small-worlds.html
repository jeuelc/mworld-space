Small Worlds

What do you know about the solar system? You can see the sun, moon and some planets in the sky with your eyes or with binoculars, and you can see close-up pictures of them taken with telescopes and spacecraft. But there's a lot more to the solar system than that!

It's full of millions of small worlds and smaller rocky fragments. Some are the size of countries like yours and others are the size of a pin-head! What are these small solar system bodies?
